Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2025

This privacy policy describes how Car Modify App ("Application", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information. By using our Application, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services.

a. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Our application uses anonymous authentication through Firebase. When you first use the app, an anonymous account is automatically created for you. We do not collect personal information such as email addresses, names, or phone numbers unless you explicitly choose to provide them through optional features.
  • Tarot Reading Data: When you perform tarot card readings, we collect and store the following information: the cards drawn, reading type (e.g., daily card, love reading, career guidance), interpretations and analysis results, timestamps, and any custom notes or preferences you provide.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide optional profile information such as display name and profile photo, which are stored in your anonymous account.

b. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect usage data through Firebase Analytics, including your interactions within the application, the screens you view, the features you use, and session duration. This data is anonymized and used to improve app performance and user experience.
  • Push Notification Token (FCM Token): To send you push notifications, we collect and store a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token generated specifically for your device. This token is stored in your anonymous user profile in our database.
  • Analytics Data: We collect anonymous usage analytics through Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app, improve app performance, and enhance user experience. This data does not include personally identifiable information.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash reports and error logs through Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix technical issues. These reports may include device information, app version, and error details, but do not include personal information.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support and analytics purposes.
  • Ad Interaction Data: When you interact with advertisements displayed in our app (through Google Mobile Ads), ad networks may collect information about your interactions. This is governed by their respective privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide the core functionalities of the application, including tarot card readings, card interpretations, reading history, and personalized insights based on your past readings.
  • Personalization: To personalize and improve your application experience, including deck style preferences, notification settings, and reading recommendations based on your usage patterns.
  • Communication: To send you service-related updates, push notifications about daily cards or reading reminders, and respond to your support requests.
  • Ad Display: To display advertisements in our app through Google Mobile Ads, which helps us provide the app free of charge.
  • Analysis and Development: To analyze the application's performance, fix bugs through crash reports, and develop new features based on usage analytics.
  • AI Processing: To process your tarot reading data through our AI analysis system to provide personalized interpretations and insights for your card readings.
  • Reading History: To store and display your past tarot readings, allowing you to review your reading history and track your spiritual journey over time.
  • Account Management: To manage your anonymous user account and maintain your profile information and reading history.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties without your consent. However, we may share information with the following services to provide our services:

  • Google Services: For analytics (Firebase Analytics), cloud storage (Firebase Storage), database services (Cloud Firestore), and advertising services (Google Mobile Ads).
  • Firebase/Google Cloud: For data storage (Firestore), anonymous authentication (Firebase Auth), push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging), crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics), remote configuration, and app functionality.
  • AI Services: Your tarot reading data (card selections and reading context) may be processed by third-party AI services through Firebase AI to provide tarot card interpretations and personalized insights.
  • Google Mobile Ads: For displaying advertisements in our app. Ad networks may collect information about your ad interactions, which is governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • Tarot API Services: We use external tarot card APIs (tarotapi.dev) to fetch tarot card information and meanings. No personal information is shared with these services.

4. Data Security and Retention

We implement industry-standard technical and administrative security measures to ensure the security of your data. Your tarot reading data and personal information are encrypted and stored securely using Firebase security measures, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and secure authentication protocols. Your data is retained for as long as necessary to provide our services or to comply with our legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and associated data, including all saved tarot reading data, at any time by contacting us via email at the address provided below. Upon receiving your deletion request, we will permanently remove all your personal data, including reading history, from our systems.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have various rights over your data. You can access and update the personal information associated with your account or request to delete your account. Upon an account deletion request, your data will be permanently removed from our systems, including all tarot readings, reading history, and personal content.

  • Data Access: You can view your tarot reading history within the app. Your reading history is stored in your anonymous account and accessible through the app interface.
  • Data Correction: You can update your optional profile information (display name, profile photo) through the app's profile settings at any time.
  • Data Deletion: You can request complete deletion of your anonymous account and all associated data, including all saved tarot reading data, by contacting us via email at the address provided below. Since accounts are anonymous, you may need to provide your device's unique identifier or other information to help us locate and delete your account data. We will process your deletion request within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Notification Preferences: You can control push notification settings through the app's notification settings, including the ability to disable notifications entirely.
  • Analytics Opt-Out: You can disable analytics data collection through your device settings, though this may affect our ability to improve the app experience.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction, such as 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at the email address provided below, and we will take steps to delete such information from our systems.
